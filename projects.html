Smart Sensor-Based Water Quality Monitoring System

This project involves designing a Smart Water Quality Monitoring System to ensure clean and safe water by continuously monitoring key parameters. Utilizing sensors for pH, Total Dissolved Solids (TDS), water flow, and water level, the system provides real-time data directly on-site. The Arduino Uno processes the sensor inputs, displaying critical information on an LCD screen and triggering alerts for any deviations. By offering an accessible and cost-effective solution, this system addresses the challenge of water quality management, particularly in areas where traditional testing methods are impractical. The successful implementation demonstrates the project's potential to enhance public health and water resource management.
